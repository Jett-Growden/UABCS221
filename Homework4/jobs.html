Work at JavaJam

Barista

We're looking for energetic and passionate individuals to join our team as Baristas. As a Barista at JavaJam, you'll be responsible for creating delicious coffee and providing excellent customer service. Join us in crafting the perfect coffee experience!

  • Prepare and serve a variety of coffee drinks
  • Interact with customers, take orders, and handle payments
  • Maintain a clean and organized workspace
  • Provide information about our coffee offerings

Hourly Wage: $10.50

Kitchen Staff

If you have a passion for cooking and want to be part of our kitchen team, consider applying for the Kitchen Staff position at JavaJam. As a Kitchen Staff member, you'll play a crucial role in preparing our delicious food items and maintaining kitchen cleanliness.

  • Prepare and cook food items following our recipes
  • Ensure food safety and hygiene standards are met
  • Assist with food inventory management
  • Collaborate with other kitchen staff to ensure smooth operations

Hourly Wage: $11.00
